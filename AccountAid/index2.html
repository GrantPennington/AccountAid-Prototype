Our vision

Our vision for AccountAid is to provide accounting tools to people. We want to provide an interface for users to explore their options for banking, budgeting, etc. Strategies to keep track of money should be available to all currency-wielding individuals or groups. Why not have all your tools in the same place?!

Who is this for?

AccountAid is intended to support ANY individual or group! Our intuitive interface has been developed to encourage all people to take charge of their finances. Whether you are a student, a budding business, or a well-known company, AccountAid is here to lend a hand.

What is AccountAid?

Finding a way to balance your finances can be difficult in more ways than one! AccountAid puts every tool you need at your fingertips. From banking and monetary transactions to budgeting, our application encompasses the basics for everyone!

Key Features

  • Budget Buddy

    holds you accountable for your money! Set personal goals for saving and even set restrictions on how much you can spend! Budget Buddy is your go to assistant for helping you keep more of your hard earned cash!

  • Digital Wallet

    will help you manage your finaces in your own personal account where you can make payments, transfer funds, and all the features you'd expect in virtual banking!

  • Personal Dashboard

    puts all of your banking needs in one area. See how and where you are spending your money. See when your monthly payments are due and keep track of your money
